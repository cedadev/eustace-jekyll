<ul></ul>
<h3><b>A</b></h3>
<p>AASTI                                         Arctic and Antarctic Ice Surface Temperatures from thermal Infrared satellite sensors</p>
<p>AIRS                                           Atmospheric InfraRed Sounder</p>
<p>Air temperature                           See page on <a href="https://www.eustaceproject.org/users/why-a-new-temperature-dataset/">"<em><strong>Why a new temperature dataset" </strong></em></a></p>
<p>ARM                                            Atmospheric Radiation Measurement programme</p>
<p>Average temperature                  In station data files often the average temperature based on hourly temperature data is presented (the average and mean temperatures may differ)</p>
<h3><strong>C</strong></h3>
<p>CCI                                             Climate Change Initiative</p>
<p>CDG                                           Climate Data Guide (<em><strong><a href="https://climatedataguide.ucar.edu/" target="_blank">https://climatedataguide.ucar.edu/</a></strong></em>)</p>
<p>CMSAF                                       EUMETSAT’s Climate and Monitoring, Satellite Application Facility</p>
<p>CEDA                                         Centre for Environmental Data Analysis</p>
<p>CEMS                                         Climate and Environmental Monitoring from Space</p>
<h3><strong>D<br></strong></h3>
<p>DEM                                            Digital Elevation Model</p>
<p>DMI                                             Danish Meteorological Institute</p>
<p>DOI                                             Digital Object Identifiers</p>
<p>DTR                                            Diurnal Temperature Range</p>
<h3><strong>E</strong></h3>
<p>EC                                              European Commission</p>
<p>ECA&amp;D                                       European Climate Assessement Database  <em><strong>(</strong></em><a href="http://www.ecad.eu/" target="_blank"><em><strong>https://www.ecad.eu</strong></em>/</a>)</p>
<p>ECV                                            Essential Climate Variables</p>
<p>E-OBS                                        Gridded dataset bases on data from ECA&amp;D (<em><strong><a href="https://www.ecad.eu/download/ensembles/ensembles.php" target="_blank">https://www.ecad.eu/download/ensembles/ensembles.php</a></strong></em>)</p>
<p>ERA                                            ECMWF Reanalysis</p>
<p>ESA                                            European Space Agency</p>
<p>ESA CCI                                     European Space Agency  Climate Change Initiative</p>
<p>EUMETSAT                                European Organisation for the Exploitation of Meteorological Satellites</p>
<p>EUSTACE                                   EU Surface Temperature for All Corners of Earth</p>
<h3><strong>F</strong></h3>
<p>FVC                                            Fraction of Vegetation Cover</p>
<h3><strong>G</strong></h3>
<p>GHCN-D                                     Global Historical Climate Network – Daily</p>
<p>GlobTemp                                   GlobTemperature project (<em><strong><a href="http://www.globtemperature.info/" target="_blank">http://www.globtemperature.info/</a></strong></em>)</p>
<p>GSOD                                         Global Surface Summary of the Day </p>
<h3><strong>H</strong></h3>
<p>HadISD                                       Hadley Centre Integrated Surface Daily</p>
<h3><strong>I</strong></h3>
<p>IAT                                              Near surface air temperatures over ice</p>
<p>ICA&amp;D                                        International Climate Assessment Database  (<em><strong><a href="https://www.ecad.eu//icad.php">https://www.ecad.eu/icad.php</a></strong></em>)</p>
<p>ICOADS                                     International Comprehensive Ocean-Atmosphere Data Set</p>
<p>IPCC                                           Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change</p>
<p>IPCC DDC                                  IPCC Data Distribution Centre</p>
<p>IST                                              Ice Surface Temperature</p>
<h3><strong>J</strong></h3>
<p>JULES                                        Joint UK Land Environment Simulator</p>
<h3><strong>K</strong></h3>
<p>KNMI                                           Royal Dutch Meteorological Institute (Koninklijk Nederlands Meteorologisch Instituut)</p>
<h3><strong>L</strong></h3>
<p>Locally systematic uncertainty    Errors that are highly correlated across short separations in time and distance (see Product user guide section 2.1.2.1)</p>
<p>LSA-SAF                                     EUMETSAT’s Land Surface Analysis Satellite Application Facility</p>
<p>LSAT                                           Land Surface Air Temperature</p>
<p>LST                                             Land Surface Temperature</p>
<p>LSTday                                       Daytime LST</p>
<p>LSTngt                                        Night time LST</p>
<p>LSWT                                          Lake Surface Water Temperature</p>
<h3><strong>M</strong></h3>
<p>MAD                                           Mean Absolute Difference</p>
<p>MAT                                            Marine Air Temperature</p>
<p>Maximum temperature                Highest temperature measured during a day, often measured in the early afternoon</p>
<p>MDB                                            Matchup DataBase   </p>
<p>Mean temperature                       Average of the minimum and maximum daily temperature (over land)  In station data files often the average temperature based on hourly temperature</p>
<p>                                                    data is presented. The average and mean temperatures may differ.</p>
<p>Minimum temperature                 Lowest temperature measured during a day, often measured in the early morning just before sunrise</p>
<p>MODIS                                        MODerate resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer (satellite)</p>
<h3><strong>N</strong></h3>
<p>NACLIM                                      North Atlantic CLIMate (project) </p>
<p>NASA                                          National Aeronautics and Space Administration</p>
<p>NH                                              Northern Hemisphere</p>
<p>NMAT                                          Night time Marine Air Temperature</p>
<p>NWP                                           Numerical Weather Prediction</p>
<h3><strong>O</strong></h3>
<p>OSI-SAF                                     EUMETSAT’s Ocean and Sea Ice Satellite Application Facility</p>
<h3><strong>P</strong></h3>
<p>PROMICE                                   PROGramme for Monitoring of the Greenland ICE Sheet</p>
<h3><strong>Q</strong></h3>
<p>QC                                              Quality Control</p>
<h3><strong>R</strong></h3>
<p>Random uncertainty                   Errors that are both random and independent between all data (see Product user guide section 2.1.2.1)        </p>
<p>RMSD                                         Root Mean Square Difference</p>
<h3><strong>S</strong></h3>
<p>SAF                                             Satellite Application Facilities</p>
<p>SAT                                             Surface Air Temperature</p>
<p>SEVIRI                                        Spinning Enhanced Visible and InfraRed Imager (satellite)</p>
<p>SH                                               Southern Hemisphere</p>
<p>Skin temperature                         See surface skin temperature  </p>
<p>SST                                            Sea Surface Temperature</p>
<p>STFC                                          Science &amp; Technology Facilities Council</p>
<p>Surface air temperature              Air temperature at a height of about 2 meters (see "<a href="https://www.eustaceproject.org/users/why-a-new-temperature-dataset/"><em><strong>Why a new temperature dataset</strong></em></a>")     </p>
<p>Surface skin temperature           Temperature of the surface of the earth measured by satelittes (see "<a href="https://www.eustaceproject.org/users/why-a-new-temperature-dataset/"><em><strong>Why a new temperature dataset</strong></em></a>")     </p>
<p>Systematic uncertainty               Errors that have a structure that is persistent in time and space (see Product user guide section 2.1.2.1)             </p>
<p>SZA                                            Solar Zenith Angle</p>
<h3><strong>T</strong></h3>
<p>T2m                                            Screen-level air temperature (at 2 m height, normally measured at in situ station)</p>
<p>Tasmax                                       Maximum surface air temperature (= Tmax)</p>
<p>Tasmin                                        Minimum surface air temperature (=Tmin)</p>
<p>Tavg                                            Averaged daily T2m</p>
<p>Tmax                                           Maximum daily T2m</p>
<p>Tmean                                        See mean temperature</p>
<p>Tmin                                            Minimum daily T2m</p>
<p>Tskin                                           Skin temperature (measured with satellite)</p>
<h3><strong>U</strong></h3>
<p>Uncertainty                                   The ‘doubt’ we have about the measurand’s value, given the measured value and our understanding of effects causing errors (see Product user guide section 2.1.2.1)</p>
<h3><strong>W</strong></h3>
<p>WP                                                 Work package</p>
